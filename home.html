About Braegen Monitor

Braegen Monitor is a unique platform designed to offer tailored solutions for children with special needs. By providing personalized activities, meditation sessions, and EEG report analysis, we aim to create an engaging and supportive environment for both children and caretakers. Our vision is to enhance the well-being of children through innovative technology and professional psychiatric care.

Why Choose Us?

Professional Support

We provide virtual consultations with child psychiatrists to ensure expert care.

Innovative Technology

Our AI-powered platform offers personalized activities and real-time feedback.

Seamless Integration

EEG reports can be submitted directly through the app for easy diagnosis.
